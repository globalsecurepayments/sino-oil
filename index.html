Innovative Recovery Technologies

We deploy a suite of proprietary technologies designed to tackle the most complex reservoir challenges and significantly increase asset value.

Polymer Flooding

Polymer Flooding

Improves sweep efficiency in reservoirs by increasing water viscosity, pushing more oil towards production wells. Ideal for mature fields.

Gas Injection Systems

Gas Injection Systems

Utilizes CO2 or other gases to expand the oil and reduce its viscosity, enabling easier and more substantial recovery from deep formations.

Chemical EOR

Chemical EOR

Employs specialized surfactants and alkalis to reduce interfacial tension between oil and water, mobilizing trapped oil that is otherwise unrecoverable.
